Your Impact

See how your contributions have transformed communities and helped improve the environment:

Story 1: Clean Beach Initiative

Thanks to your generous donations, we've cleaned over 50 km of beaches, removing thousands of tons of waste. This has improved marine life and created safer coastal environments.

Story 2: Community Recycling Program

Your support has helped establish community recycling centers in 20 cities, improving waste management for over 1 million people. These centers play a crucial role in reducing waste and promoting sustainability.

Story 3: Eco-friendly Education

With your help, we've educated over 100,000 students on sustainable practices, empowering the next generation to protect the environment and make eco-conscious choices in their daily lives.
